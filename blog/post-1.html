Why I Teach Mathematics the Way I Do

9 July 2025

Home Back to Blog Index

I've been teaching mathematics for over six years, and the way I approach it has changed dramatically over time. I used to focus on rigour above all else, but I’ve learned that accessibility, curiosity, and context are just as important — especially for students who don’t yet see themselves as ‘maths people’.

In this post, I want to reflect on what shaped my teaching, how I scaffold abstract ideas, and what I've learned from student feedback.
